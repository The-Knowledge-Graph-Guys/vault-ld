# Culinary
The minimal example ontology from Appendix A of [[Vault-LD Specification - Linked Data in the Vault]]. It defines a tiny domain — recipes, the creative works they specialise, and the ingredients they require — just rich enough to show classes, a subclass edge, an object property, and a datatype property living entirely in frontmatter.
